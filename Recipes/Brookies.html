How to make Brookies!

A brookie is part brownie, part cookie, and totally delicious.You'll find step-by-step instructions to make your first batch of Brookies in the recipe below:

  1. Make the cookie dough and press it into a greased baking dish.
  2. Make the brownie batter and spread it over the cookie layer.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
  4. Cut the brookies into bars.
  5. Store the brookies in an airtight container at room temperature.
  6. Congratulations on your first batch of brookies you are done!
